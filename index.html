Geomagnetic Storms Education


Here you will learn about geomagnetic storms, a fascinating phenomenon
that occurs in space but directly affects Earth! Here, you will understand in
a simple and engaging way how these storms occur, what events on the
Sun are necessary to trigger these bursts of energy and the impact they
can have on our planet, such as beautiful auroras or even problems with
satellites and power grids. If you are a student curious about the universe
and want to learn more about the myst eries of space and how it influences
our daily lives, this is the right place!
