Geomagnetic Storms Education


Here you will learn about geomagnetic storms, a fascinating phenomenon
that occurs in space but directly affects Earth! Here, you'll understand in a
simple and engaging way how these storms happen, which events on the
Sun are necessary to trigger these explosions of energy, and the impact they
can have on our planet, such as beautiful auroras or even problems with
satellites and power grids. If you're a student curious about the universe
and want to learn more about the mysteries of space and how it influences
our daily lives, this is the right place!
